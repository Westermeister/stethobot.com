Diabetes Diagnosis

Prerequisites

In order to diagnose diabetes, Stethobot needs the following 8 values:

  1. Number of times the patient has been pregnant.
  2. Plasma glucose concentration, 2 hours in an oral glucose tolerance test.
  3. Diastolic blood pressure (mmHg).
  4. Skin thickness i.e. triceps skin fold thickness (mm).
  5. Insulin i.e. 2-hour serum insulin (muU/ml).
  6. BMI i.e. body mass index.
  7. Diabetes pedigree function.
  8. Age in years.

If any of this is unclear in any way, please check out the source of the dataset used to train this model. Since the dataset originates from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases, you may also direct inquiries there. Additionally, in the input fields below, example values are provided.

All fields are required.
