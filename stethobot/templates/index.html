Stethobot

computer-aided diagnosis

GET STARTED

What is this?

Stethobot is a proof-of-concept website designed to help medical professionals diagnose their patients with certain medical conditions. Stethobot is not intended for real-world usage.

The idea behind Stethobot is simple. A medical professional first collects various measurements about a patient. What these measurements are depends on the specific medical condition that the professional wants to test for, and can include values such as BMI, blood pressure, or age. Then, these values are sent to Stethobot's servers, which run powerful machine learning algorithms on the given data to generate a predicted diagnosis.

Why Stethobot?

There are a number of reasons that medical professionals might take a look at Stethobot:

  • Accuracy. Stethobot's diagnostic models are up to 95% accurate and 88% accurate overall.
  • Quality assurance. You will never see any model on Stethobot that doesn't possess at least 80% accuracy, and the overall average across models will never drop below 85%.
  • Extra statistics. In addition to providing each model's accuracy, Stethobot also provides the sensitivity and specificity, so you can properly gauge a model's likelihood to produce false positives and false negatives.
  • Trusted algorithms. Stethobot makes heavy use of Balanced Random Forests, which are an upgraded variant of the well-known and highly-performative Random Forest algorithm. Balanced Random Forests are more resilient to the common issue of unbalanced data in medical datasets. And speaking of datasets...
  • Transparency. Stethobot's models are trained off of well-known and publicly-available datasets contained the UC Irvine Machine Learning Repository. So if you're ever in doubt of a model, you can check the dataset it was trained with. In addition, Stethobot also lists the authors of each dataset and their contact information.
  • Privacy. The data you send to Stethobot is not stored. Period.
  • Free and account-less. You don't have to pay to use Stethobot, and you don't have to register for an account. Neither will you be burdened with advertisements: Stethobot is a passion project, not a commercial one.
  • Simple and streamlined. Forget the auto-playing videos, pop-up ads, hidden menus, and other annoyances that many other modern websites employ. Stethobot's UX is simple, clean, fast, and to-the-point.

Why not Stethobot?

As aforementioned, Stethobot is a proof-of-concept and a passion project at its core. It wasn't made with serious, real-world use in mind.

This point is not brought up to downplay Stethobot's capabilities. Rather, it is simply to strongly encourage any medical professional that is considering serious usage of this website in a real-world application to exercise reasonable caution. For instance, they should...

  • ...thoroughly investigate the datasets used to train the models that they wish to use.
  • ...ensure that they have all the necessary resources to gather the values that Stethobot requires to make a prediction.
  • ...compare Stethobot's diagonses with that of their fellow medical professionals in order to ascertain its viability.
